Twelve specialities, one roof

Comprehensive dental care under one roof, from preventive checkups to full mouth rehabilitation.

no. 01

Diagnosis & Consultation

Thorough clinical examination and dental X-rays to accurately diagnose your condition and plan the best course of treatment.

no. 02

Restorations & Fillings

Amalgam (silver) and tooth-coloured composite fillings to restore decayed or damaged teeth, durable, natural-looking results for all cavity classes.

no. 03

Crowns & Bridges

Metal, PFM, Zirconia and E-Max crowns, along with fixed bridges and laminates, precision-crafted to restore strength and aesthetics.

no. 04

Root Canal Treatment

Painless endodontic therapy including post & core buildup and apicectomy, saving infected teeth and eliminating pain with minimal discomfort. Completed in a single visit.

no. 05

Orthodontics & Aligners

Fixed braces, removable orthodontic appliances and clear aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues at any age.

no. 06

Gum Surgery & Periodontics

Scaling, subgingival cleaning, periodontal surgery and apicectomy, comprehensive care to treat gum disease and protect your oral foundation.

no. 07

Dental Implants

Basic and premium implant systems for permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement, restoring full function and confidence.

no. 08

Dentures & Prosthetics

Removable partial dentures (RPD), complete dentures in basic and premium options, and cast partial dentures, precision-fitted for comfort.

no. 09

Oral Surgery & Extractions

Basic and surgical extractions, impaction removal, milk tooth extractions, incision & drainage, and frenectomy, gentle and precise.

no. 10

Pedodontics: Children's Dentistry

Paediatric crowns, pit & fissure sealants, fluoride application, space maintainers, pulpotomy and habit-breaking appliances for growing smiles.

no. 11

Teeth Bleaching & Whitening

In-office professional bleaching and take-home whitening kits to brighten your smile by several shades safely and effectively.

no. 12

Preventive Care & Scaling

Routine scaling per sitting, fluoride treatments and oral hygiene education to maintain long-term dental health and prevent future problems.

    ( 05 ) Advanced technology

    Instruments of precision

    We invest in world-class dental technology to deliver precise, comfortable and superior treatment outcomes.

    Advanced Surgical Technology
    Physiodispenser
    Electronically controlled surgical motor for precise, safe dental implant placement with integrated irrigation cooling.
    Dio UV Light Activator
    Award-winning UV curing unit ensuring optimal polymerization for composite restorations.
    iF Design Award 2020
    Regenerative Dentistry
    PRF Centrifuge
    Spins a small sample of your own blood to produce a natural, growth-factor-rich concentrate that dramatically accelerates healing after procedures.
    Electrosurgery Unit
    Radio frequency electrosurgery system for precise soft tissue management and bloodless surgical procedures.
    LED Curing Light
    High-intensity LED curing light for fast, reliable polymerization of light-cured dental materials.
    Portable Imaging
    Genoray Port-X III Portable X-Ray
    Lightweight handheld X-ray system for sharp intra-oral images at the chairside, with 0.5T lead shielding for patient and practitioner safety.
    Class B Sterilization
    Fussen Autoclave: 23L Class B
    Hospital-grade Class B steam autoclave with a 23-litre chamber, delivering fully validated sterilization of all wrapped and hollow instruments.
    UV Sterilization Chamber
    Ultraviolet storage cabinet that keeps sterilized instruments contaminant-free until the moment they are used.
    Ultrasonic Cleaner
    Heated ultrasonic bath that uses high-frequency waves to remove debris from instruments before sterilization, cleaning where a brush cannot reach.
    Fussen Sealing Machine
    Seals instruments into sterilization pouches with a secure, tamper-evident seam, maintaining a sterile barrier right up to the point of use.
    Distilled Water Machine
    Produces pure distilled water in-house for the autoclave, protecting the sterilizer from scale and ensuring every cycle runs on clean, mineral-free water.
    Dental Implant Systems
    Premium implant kits from globally certified brands, ensuring long-term strength, biocompatibility and osseointegration.
    3D Intraoral Scanner
    Digital impression scanning for accurate 3D tooth models, eliminating messy putty impressions entirely.
