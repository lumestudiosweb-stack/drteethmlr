What We Offer

Comprehensive care,
under one roof

From preventive checkups to full mouth rehabilitation, twelve specialist treatments delivered with genuine care.

Diagnosis & Consultation

Thorough clinical examination and dental X-rays to accurately diagnose your condition and plan the best treatment.

Restorations & Fillings

Tooth-coloured composite and amalgam fillings that restore decayed teeth with durable, natural-looking results.

Crowns & Bridges

Metal, PFM, Zirconia and E-Max crowns with fixed bridges and laminates, precision-crafted for strength and aesthetics.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless endodontic therapy that saves infected teeth and eliminates pain. Completed in a single visit.

Orthodontics & Aligners

Fixed braces, removable appliances and clear aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues at any age.

Gum Surgery & Periodontics

Scaling, subgingival cleaning and periodontal surgery to treat gum disease and protect your oral foundation.

Dental Implants

Basic and premium implant systems for permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement that restores full function.

Dentures & Prosthetics

Removable partial, complete and cast partial dentures, precision-fitted for everyday comfort.

Oral Surgery & Extractions

Surgical extractions, impaction removal, incision & drainage and frenectomy, gentle and precise.

Pedodontics

Paediatric crowns, fissure sealants, fluoride application and habit-breaking appliances for growing smiles.

Teeth Whitening

In-office professional bleaching and take-home kits to brighten your smile safely by several shades.

Preventive Care & Scaling

Routine scaling, fluoride treatments and hygiene education to keep problems from ever starting.

Advanced Technology

World-class equipment

Portable Imaging

Genoray Port-X III X-Ray

Lightweight handheld X-ray producing sharp intra-oral images, with 0.5T lead shielding for patient and practitioner safety.

Class B Sterilization

Fussen Autoclave — 23L

Hospital-grade Class B steam autoclave delivering fully validated sterilization of all wrapped and hollow instruments.

Surgical Technology

Physiodispenser

Electronically controlled surgical motor for precise, safe implant placement with integrated irrigation cooling.

Regenerative Dentistry

PRF Centrifuge

Produces a natural, growth-factor-rich concentrate from your own blood that dramatically accelerates healing.

Digital Dentistry

3D Intraoral Scanner

Digital impression scanning for accurate 3D tooth models, eliminating messy putty impressions entirely.

Sterile Storage

UV Sterilization Chamber

Ultraviolet cabinet that keeps sterilized instruments contaminant-free until the moment they are used.

Instrument Hygiene

Ultrasonic Cleaner

Heated ultrasonic bath using high-frequency waves to clean instruments where a brush cannot reach.

Sterile Barrier

Fussen Sealing Machine

Seals instruments into sterilization pouches with a tamper-evident seam, sterile right up to the point of use.

Pure Water

Distilled Water Machine

Produces pure distilled water in-house so every sterilization cycle runs on clean, mineral-free water.

iF Design Award 2020

Dio UV Light Activator

Award-winning UV curing unit ensuring optimal polymerization for composite restorations.

Soft Tissue Care

Electrosurgery Unit

Radio frequency system for precise soft tissue management and bloodless surgical procedures.

Restorative Care

LED Curing Light

High-intensity LED for fast, reliable polymerization of light-cured dental materials.

Certified Brands

Dental Implant Systems

Premium implant kits from globally certified brands for long-term strength and osseointegration.
