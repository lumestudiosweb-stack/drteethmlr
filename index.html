What We Offer

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care under one roof — from preventive checkups to full mouth rehabilitation.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements using state-of-the-art implant technology for a confident, lasting smile.

Teeth Whitening

Professional in-clinic whitening treatments that brighten your smile by several shades in a single session.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Veneers, bonding, smile makeovers and aesthetic enhancements designed to transform and perfect your smile.

Crowns & Bridges

Custom-crafted ceramic crowns and fixed bridges that restore function and aesthetics with precision fit.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless, precision endodontic treatment to save infected teeth and eliminate pain with minimal discomfort.

Orthodontics & Braces

Metal braces, ceramic braces and clear aligners to straighten teeth and correct bite issues at any age.

Preventive Care & Cleaning

Routine scaling, polishing, fluoride treatment and oral health education to keep your teeth healthy long-term.

Oral Surgery & Extractions

Wisdom tooth removal, surgical extractions and minor oral surgery performed with care and precision.

Dentures & Prosthetics

Complete and partial dentures, precision-fitted for comfort and a natural appearance, restoring your quality of life.

Advanced Technology

Our Equipment

We invest in world-class dental technology to deliver precise, comfortable and superior treatment outcomes.

DTE Implant Air Motor
High-precision implant motor system for accurate torque-controlled placement of dental implants.
Dio UV Light Activator
Award-winning UV curing unit ensuring optimal polymerization for composite restorations.
iF Design Award 2020
Labtech Dentifuge LD-C10
Dental centrifuge for PRF/PRP preparation, enhancing bone and soft tissue regeneration in implant procedures.
Electrosurgery Unit
Radio frequency electrosurgery system for precise soft tissue management and bloodless surgical procedures.
LED Curing Light
High-intensity LED curing light for fast, reliable polymerization of light-cured dental materials.
Fussen Autoclave & Portable X-Ray
Class B sterilization autoclave and portable digital X-ray for chairside imaging and clinical safety.
Dental Implant Systems
Premium implant kits from globally certified brands — ensuring long-term strength, biocompatibility and osseointegration.
3D Intraoral Scanner
Digital impression scanning for accurate 3D tooth models — eliminating messy putty impressions entirely.
